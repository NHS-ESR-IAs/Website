Did You Know

“Did You Know” boxes are your playful knowledge nudges—ideal for surfacing hidden features, contributor tips, or delightful facts that make onboarding memorable. They add warmth, clarity, and a touch of surprise to any layout.

  • ✨ Great for highlighting lesser-known features or contributor wisdom
  • 🎨 Theme-safe and modular—styled to harmonise across microsites
  • 🧱 Drop-in ready for cards, modals, tabs, or onboarding flows
  • 🧼 Keeps content light, engaging, and scannable

Use them to say “Did you know you can drag modals?”, “Did you know this dohicky works in dark mode?”, or “Did you know penguins debug with their flippers?”—whatever sparks joy and clarity.

💡 Did You Know Panel: Whisper a Tip
  • Update the tip: Replace the <p> content with your own joyful fact, feature highlight, or contributor wisdom.
  • Change the label: Edit the <summary> to match your tone—e.g. 💡 Did you know?, 🧠 Contributor tip, or 🎉 Surprise insight.
  • Style it safely: The .did-you-know class is theme-safe—add border, bg-light, or rounded if you want extra flair.
  • Make it interactive: Use <details> to keep the layout clean—contributors can expand when curious, skip when focused.
  • Optional: Add emojis, links, or dohickies inside the tip to make it more expressive or actionable.
