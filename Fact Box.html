Fact Box

Fact boxes are your microsite’s anchor points—ideal for surfacing technical truths, onboarding insights, or contributor-friendly reminders. They add clarity, confidence, and a touch of expressive structure to any layout.

  • 🧠 Great for highlighting modular features, accessibility support, or onboarding logic
  • 🧱 Modular and drop-in ready for dashboards, tip flows, or role-based layouts
  • 🎨 Theme-safe and expressive—styled to harmonise across microsites
  • 🧼 Keeps facts scannable, stable, and contributor-friendly

Use them to say “This microsite uses modular dohickies,” “Scroll-collapse logic is flicker-free,” or “Accessibility flags are active”—whatever helps contributors feel informed and empowered.

📦 Fact: This microsite uses modular dohickies to make onboarding joyful.
📦 Fact Box Panel: Drop in the Truth
  • Change the fact: Edit the text to highlight a new technical insight, onboarding feature, or contributor-friendly truth.
  • Swap the emoji: Replace 📦 with 🧠, 🔍, 🧱, or any expressive icon that suits your tone.
  • Style it safely: Use theme-safe classes like factbox, border, bg-light, or rounded to harmonise with your microsite.
  • Make it modular: Nest inside cards, tabs, or onboarding flows—fact boxes add clarity wherever contributors need it.
  • Optional: Add links, dohickies, or hover effects to make the fact interactive or expandable.
