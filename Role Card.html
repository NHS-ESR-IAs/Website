Role Card

Role cards are your onboarding compass—ideal for giving contributors tailored guidance based on their role, mindset, or starting point. They help each persona feel seen, supported, and empowered to dive in with clarity.

  • 🎯 Great for developers, maintainers, editors, educators, and more
  • 🧱 Modular and drop-in ready for dashboards, onboarding flows, or role-based layouts
  • 🎨 Theme-safe and expressive—styled to harmonise across microsites
  • 🧼 Keeps guidance scannable, role-specific, and contributor-friendly

Use them to say “Start with the dohickies,” “Check the scroll-collapse logic,” or “Review the accessibility flags”—whatever helps each contributor find their flow.

🧑‍💻 Role Card Panel: Guide by Persona
  • Change the role: Edit the <h3> to match the contributor’s persona—e.g. 🧑‍💻 Developer, 🧑‍🏫 Educator, or 🧑‍🔧 Maintainer.
  • Update the guidance: Modify the <p> to share role-specific tips, starting points, or onboarding nudges.
  • Style it safely: Use theme-safe classes like role-card, border, bg-light, or rounded to harmonise with your microsite.
  • Make it modular: Stack multiple role cards in a grid, carousel, or tabbed layout to guide contributors by context.
  • Optional: Add emojis, links, or dohickies to make the card more expressive or interactive.
