Welcome to MicroSiteKit 🧱✨

Your modular toolkit for building joyful, contributor-first microsites—no CMS stress, no flicker, just pure layout clarity.

Whether you're launching a training hub, onboarding portal, or snippet showcase, MicroSiteKit gives you everything you need to scaffold expressive, theme-safe pages with ease. Think of it as your digital LEGO set—stackable, swappable, and built for clarity.

🧭 What’s a Microsite?

A microsite is a focused, standalone page or mini-hub—perfect for showcasing a role, a resource, or a training journey. It’s lightweight, fast-loading, and designed to guide contributors with purpose.

🎨 What’s in the Kit?
  • 🧩 Layout Scaffolds: Header, Footer, Navigation, Side Menu, Page Code
  • 📦 Content Blocks: Fact Box, Tip Box, Quote, Image, Carousel, Did You Know?
  • 🧑‍💻 Contributor Cards: Role Card, Shout Out Card
  • 🎛️ Interactive Dohickies: Modal, Toggle Section, Tabs, Alerts
  • ♿ Accessibility Tools: Accessibility Flag, Update Banner, Dark Mode Toggle
  • 🚀 Navigation Launchers: Open New Page Button, Pop Out Page Button

Each snippet is theme-safe, copy-paste ready, and wrapped in warm scaffolding to help contributors feel confident and empowered. You can mix, match, and drop them into any microsite page—no CMS required.
