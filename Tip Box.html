Tip Box

Tip boxes are your onboarding whispers—perfect for surfacing smart moves, hidden shortcuts, or contributor-friendly nudges that make technical spaces feel welcoming. They offer clarity without clutter and empower contributors to explore with confidence.

  • 🎯 Great for sharing modular tricks, role-based guidance, or CMS-safe workarounds
  • 🧱 Modular and drop-in ready for dashboards, onboarding flows, or dohicky grids
  • 🎨 Theme-safe and expressive—styled to harmonise across microsites
  • 🧼 Keeps tips scannable, optional, and contributor-friendly

Use them to say “Use .dohicky-* classes to drop in modular features,” “Check onboarding.js for scroll-collapse logic,” or “Role cards work best inside a grid”—whatever sparks clarity and contributor joy.

🛠 Contributor Tip

Use `.dohicky-*` classes to drop in modular features without touching the CMS.

🛠 Tip Box Panel: Nudge with Know-How
  • Change the tip: Edit the <p> to share a new insight, shortcut, or contributor-friendly trick.
  • Update the label: Modify the <summary> to match your tone—e.g. 🛠 Contributor Tip, 🧠 Smart Move, or 🎯 Quick Win.
  • Style it safely: Use theme-safe classes like tip-box, border, bg-light, or rounded to harmonise with your microsite.
  • Make it modular: Nest inside cards, tabs, or onboarding flows—tips add clarity wherever contributors need it.
  • Optional: Add emojis, links, or dohickies inside the tip to make it more expressive or actionable.
