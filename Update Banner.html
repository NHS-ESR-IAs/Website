Update Banner

Update banners are your changelog cheerleaders—ideal for announcing fixes, feature releases, or behind-the-scenes improvements that make the contributor experience smoother. They keep your microsite transparent, joyful, and flicker-free.

  • 🚀 Great for surfacing bug fixes, refactors, and new dohickies
  • 🧱 Modular and drop-in ready for dashboards, onboarding flows, or role-based layouts
  • 🎨 Theme-safe and expressive—styled to harmonise across microsites
  • 🧼 Keeps updates visible, scannable, and contributor-friendly

Use them to say “Scroll-collapse logic refactored for flicker-free transitions,” “New tip-box added for maintainers,” or “Factbox now supports emoji!”—whatever sparks clarity and celebration.

🔄 Update Banner Panel: Surface the Change
  • Change the message: Edit the text to reflect a new update, bug fix, or feature release—keep it clear and contributor-friendly.
  • Swap the emoji: Replace 🔄 with 🚀, 🐛, 🎯, or any expressive icon that suits your changelog tone.
  • Style it safely: Use theme-safe classes like update-banner, border, bg-light, or rounded to harmonise with your microsite.
  • Make it modular: Drop into cards, dashboards, or onboarding flows—update banners help contributors stay informed with joy.
  • Optional: Add links to release notes, dohickies, or role-based highlights for deeper context.
