Shout Out Card

Shout-out cards are your spotlight stage—perfect for recognising contributors, surfacing team wins, or adding a burst of appreciation to your microsite. They bring warmth, visibility, and a personal touch to technical spaces.

  • 🎉 Great for celebrating achievements, milestones, or helpful nudges
  • 🧱 Modular and drop-in ready for dashboards, onboarding flows, or role-based layouts
  • 🎨 Theme-safe and expressive—styled to harmonise across microsites
  • 🧼 Keeps recognition visible, joyful, and scannable

Use them to say “Shout-out to Sam for debugging the modal flicker!”, “Thanks to Priya for refactoring the dohicky!”, or “Big ups to the penguin dev who slid past scroll-collapse logic.”

🌟 Shout-out: Thanks to Bronwyn for refactoring the scroll-collapse logic!
📣 Shout-Out Panel: Shine the Spotlight
  • Change the message: Edit the text inside the card to spotlight a different contributor, achievement, or joyful moment.
  • Swap the emoji: Replace 🌟 with 🎉, 👏, 🧠, or any expressive icon that suits your tone.
  • Style it safely: Add theme-safe classes like bg-light, border, rounded, or shadow-sm to harmonise with your microsite.
  • Make it modular: Nest it inside a card, grid, or carousel to rotate shout-outs by role, team, or onboarding step.
  • Optional: Add a photo, link, or dohicky next to the name for extra flair—e.g. <img> avatar or <a> to their contribution.
