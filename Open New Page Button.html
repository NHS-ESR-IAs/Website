Open New Page Button

This button is your microsite’s modular navigator—perfect for launching snippet pages, onboarding flows, or dohicky dashboards with clarity and ease. It’s expressive, accessible, and contributor-friendly.

  • 🧭 Great for linking to snippet pages like FactBox, TipBox, or RoleCard
  • 🧱 Modular and stackable—drop into grids, menus, or onboarding flows
  • 🎨 Theme-safe and layout-friendly—styled with btn-primary and w-100 for full-width clarity
  • 🧼 Keeps navigation clean, scannable, and contributor-first

Use it to say “Open the Tip Box page,” “Launch the Accessibility Flag panel,” or “Explore the Dohicky Grid”—whatever sparks modular joy and onboarding clarity.

🧭 Open New Page Button Panel: Navigate with Joy
  • Change the label: Replace # with the button text—e.g. 📦 Fact Box, 🛠 Tip Box, or 🧩 Dohicky Grid.
  • Set the target: Replace ID in showPage('ID') with the unique ID of the page you want to open.
  • Style it safely: Use theme-safe classes like btn-primary, w-100, text-start, or mb-2 to harmonise with your microsite.
  • Make it modular: Stack buttons in a grid, sidebar, or dohicky dashboard to guide contributors with clarity.
  • Optional: Add emojis, icons, or hover effects to make navigation expressive and joyful. 

<button class="btn btn-primary w-100 text-start mb-2" onclick="showPage('FactBox')">📦 Fact Box</button>
