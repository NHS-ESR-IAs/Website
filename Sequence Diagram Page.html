This Mermaid block renders a sequence diagram showing a generic contributor interaction flow. You can customise the roles, actions, and messages to suit your own process.

  participant Contributor
  participant Microsite
  participant Guide
  participant SupportTeam

  Contributor->>Microsite: Opens diagram page
  Microsite->>Guide: Loads Mermaid block
  Guide-->>Contributor: Shows interaction flow
  Contributor->>Microsite: Copies diagram snippet
  Microsite->>SupportTeam: Sends contact request
  SupportTeam-->>Contributor: Responds with support
