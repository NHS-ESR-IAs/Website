Breadcrumbs

Breadcrumbs are your gentle guide through the microsite maze. They show contributors where they are, how they got there, and how to hop back—without ever feeling lost. Perfect for layered layouts, role-based navigation, or multi-step onboarding flows.

  • 🧭 Helps contributors orient themselves within nested content
  • 🔗 Provides quick links back to parent pages or sections
  • 🎨 Theme-safe and accessible—works across microsite palettes
  • 🧱 Modular and easy to drop into headers, cards, or dohickies

Whether you're guiding someone from “Home → Onboarding → Role Cards” or “Library → Snippets → Tabs”, breadcrumbs keep the journey clear and joyful.

🍞 Breadcrumb Snippet Panel: Trace the Trail
  • Update the trail: Change each <li> label to reflect your page hierarchy—e.g. Home → Library → Tabs or Dashboard → Role Cards → Editor.
  • Control interactivity: Use onclick="showPage('X')" or href="..." depending on whether you're using JavaScript routing or standard links.
  • Mark the current page: Add active and aria-current="page" to the final breadcrumb item to signal where the user is.
  • Theme-safe by default: No extra styling needed—just drop it into any microsite layout and it’ll harmonise beautifully.
  • Optional: Add icons, emojis, or role-based labels to make the trail more expressive (e.g. 🏠 Home, 🧑‍💼 Role Cards).
