Dohicky Grid

The Dohicky Grid is your visual index of delight—perfect for surfacing modular features, onboarding snippets, and contributor-friendly tools in one tidy layout. It invites exploration, sparks curiosity, and keeps your microsite scannable and joyful.

  • 🎯 Great for listing dohickies like tip boxes, shout cards, fact flags, and more
  • 🧱 Modular and stackable—add, remove, or reorder items with ease
  • 🎨 Theme-safe and layout-friendly—harmonises across microsites
  • 🧼 Keeps content clean, clickable, and contributor-first

Use it to say “Here’s what you can drop in,” “Pick your onboarding tools,” or “Explore the dohicky buffet”—whatever sparks clarity and modular joy.

🧩 Dohicky Grid Panel: Showcase with Joy
  • Change the items: Edit each <div class="dohicky-item"> to spotlight different snippets—e.g. 🗣️ Quote, ♿ Accessibility Flag, or 🔄 Update Banner.
  • Reorder or expand: Add, remove, or rearrange items to suit your layout—grids scale beautifully across roles and themes.
  • Style it safely: Use theme-safe classes like dohicky-grid, border, bg-light, or rounded to harmonise with your microsite.
  • Make it modular: Drop into onboarding flows, dashboards, or contributor portals—grids help users scan and explore with clarity.
  • Optional: Add hover effects, links, or dohicky previews inside each item for extra interactivity.
